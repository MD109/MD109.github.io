Water to Grape Juice to Milk

Introduction

Magically turn water to grape juice to lemonade to 7-Up to milk to finally—after consuming all that, you’ll surely need— Pepto-Bismol!

Materials

8-10mL Barium nitrate solution

3-4 drops Phenolphthalein solution

1g Sodium bicarbonate

10 drops 0.1M Sodium hydroxide solution

5-6mL 6M Sodium hydroxide solution

1.5mL Sulfuric acid solution

200mL Distilled Water

5 Beryl Pipets

6 400mL Beakers

Procedure

1. In Glass 1, combine 200mL of water with 3-4 drops of Phenolphthalein

2. In Glass 2, add 10 drops of 0.1M Sodium Hydroxide

3. In Glass 3, add 1.5mL of Sulfuric Acid

4. In Glass 4, combine 1g of Sodium Bicarbonate with 2-3mL of Water

5. In Glass 5, add 7-9mL of Barium Nitrate

6. In Glass 6, add 5-6mL of 6M Sodium Hydroxide

7. Pour the contents of Glass 1 into Glass 2.

8. Pour the contents of Glass 2 into Glass 3.

9. Pour the contents of Glass 3 into Glass 4.

10. Pour the contents of Glass 4 into Glass 5.

11. Pour the contents of Glass 5 into Glass 6.
